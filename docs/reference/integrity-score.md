# Integrity Score

Integrity score is a governance confidence indicator (0-100), not model quality.

## Signals and weights
- parityVerified: 30
- policyBound: 20
- contextCaptured: 15
- evalAttached: 10
- replayVerified: 20
- signaturePresent: 5

The score output includes a full breakdown to make every point explainable in CLI/UI.
